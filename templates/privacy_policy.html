Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Flask Chat. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Flask Chat service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password (which is securely hashed).
  • Profile Information: Any optional profile information you provide.
  • Messages: The content of messages you send through our service.
  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on our service, including login times and online status.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device and browser.

If you use Google OAuth to sign in, we receive basic profile information from Google as permitted by their privacy policy and your account settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized activity
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Send service-related notifications

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Email addresses are encrypted in our database
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS)
  • Regular security assessments of our systems

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following situations:

  • With other users as part of the normal operation of the chat service
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Restriction or objection to certain processing
  • Data portability (receiving your data in a structured format)

You can exercise many of these rights directly through your account settings or by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us to have the information removed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
