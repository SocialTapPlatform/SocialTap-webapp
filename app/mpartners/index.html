SocialTap Media Partner Program — Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: 7/30/2026

1. Eligibility
	•	The SocialTap Media Partner Program (“Program”) is open to all users aged 13 and above.
	•	Users under the age of 18 may participate with the understanding that no monetary compensation or products of value are provided.
	•	Users must have at least 500 followers on a social platform to qualify.

2. Nature of the Program
	•	The Program is a recognition-based initiative.
	•	Participants may receive up to five (5) virtual badge tiers displayed on their SocialTap profile.
	•	These badges are purely virtual symbols of recognition and carry no monetary value, rights, or compensation.

3. Partner Responsibilities
	•	Partners are encouraged to positively promote SocialTap on their social platforms.
	•	If posting about SocialTap, Partners must follow applicable advertising and disclosure guidelines, including using clear tags such as “#SocialTapPartner” where required.
	•	Partners must comply with the SocialTap Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

4. Restrictions
	•	Badges may not be sold, transferred, or exchanged.
	•	Misuse of the Program, including misleading promotions or violating platform rules, may result in removal from the Program.

5. Termination
	•	SocialTap reserves the right to suspend or terminate a participant’s status in the Program at any time, for any reason.

6. Disclaimer
	•	Participation in this Program does not create an employment, agency, or sponsorship relationship between you and SocialTap.
	•	The Program is voluntary and intended solely for recognition within the SocialTap platform.
