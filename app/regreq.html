Registration Requirements

If you're on this page, you most likely messed up something in your registration process.

Take some time to review this.

Username

  • Must be between 3 and 15 characters.
  • Only letters (a-z, A-Z) and numbers (0-9) are allowed.
  • Cannot contain blacklisted words.

Email

  • Must be a valid email address.
  • Length must be between 5 and 30 characters.

Password

  • Must be at least 6 characters long.
  • Maximum length is 30 characters.
